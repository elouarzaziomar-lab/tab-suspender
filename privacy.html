Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

No data collected

Tab Suspender does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or usage information. We have no access to what sites you visit or how you use the extension.

Local storage only

All settings, whitelists, and session data are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

No analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Segment, or any other analytics or telemetry service. There is no tracking code of any kind embedded in the extension.

No tracking servers — one optional exception

Tab Suspender makes no network requests for its core features: suspending tabs, the memory dashboard, and the whitelist all run entirely on your device. The only external connection it can ever make is to extensionpay.com, and only if you choose to buy the optional Pro upgrade — solely to process that one-time payment and verify your license. No browsing history, analytics, or telemetry is ever sent anywhere.
