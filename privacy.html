Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

No data collected

Tab Suspender does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or usage information. We have no access to what sites you visit or how you use the extension.

Local storage only

All settings, whitelists, and session data are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

No analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Segment, or any other analytics or telemetry service. There is no tracking code of any kind embedded in the extension.

No external servers

Tab Suspender makes zero network requests to external servers. The extension operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any backend service. Your browser's network tab will stay silent.
