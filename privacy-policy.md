# Privacy Policy — Tab Suspender

Last updated: June 2026

## Data collection
Tab Suspender collects no data whatsoever.

## What we don't do
- No analytics or telemetry
- No external servers
- No cookies
- No personal data collected, stored, or transmitted

## Local storage only
The only data stored is your personal settings (auto-suspend delay, 
whitelist, theme preference). This data lives exclusively on your 
device via chrome.storage.local and is never sent anywhere.
